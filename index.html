🥞 Banana Oat Pancakes

⏱️ 15 min 🍽️ 6 pancakes 👶 6+ months
finger-food freezer-friendly no-sugar

Ingredients

  • 1 ripe banana
  • 1 egg
  • ½ cup rolled oats (blitzed to flour)
  • 2 tbsp whole milk or formula
  • Pinch of cinnamon (optional)
  • Butter or coconut oil for cooking

Instructions

  1. Mash banana in a bowl until smooth
  2. Beat in the egg
  3. Add oat flour, milk, and cinnamon. Mix until combined
  4. Heat a non-stick pan over medium-low heat. Add a small amount of butter
  5. Drop spoonfuls of batter (~2 tbsp each). Cook 2 min per side until golden
  6. Cool slightly before serving — these hold heat!

Serving

Cut into strips for self-feeding. Serve with yogurt or mashed berries on the side.

Storage: Fridge 3 days · Freezer 1 month · Reheat in toaster or microwave
💡 Use very ripe banana (brown spots = sweeter). Can double batch and freeze for quick breakfasts!

🥑 Avocado Egg Mash

⏱️ 10 min 🍽️ 1 serving 👶 6+ months
no-sugar

Ingredients

  • ½ ripe avocado
  • 1 hard-boiled egg
  • 1 tbsp breast milk or formula (optional, for texture)

Instructions

  1. Hard boil egg (10 min), cool, and peel
  2. Scoop avocado into bowl
  3. Add egg and mash together with fork
  4. Add milk to thin if needed
Storage: Best fresh — avocado browns quickly. Can store 1 day with lemon juice.
💡 Great protein + healthy fats combo. Can spread on toast strips for finger food.
