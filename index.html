Banana Oat Pancakes
15 min 6 pancakes 6+ months
finger-food freezer-friendly no-sugar

Ingredients

  • 1 ripe banana
  • 1 egg
  • ½ cup rolled oats (blitzed)
  • 2 tbsp milk or formula
  • pinch of cinnamon

Method

  1. Mash banana until smooth
  2. Beat in egg, add oat flour and milk
  3. Cook spoonfuls in buttered pan, 2 min each side
  4. Cool before serving — cut into strips
fridge 3 days · freezer 1 month · reheat in toaster
Use very ripe banana (brown spots = sweeter). Double batch for freezer stash.
Avocado Egg Mash
10 min 1 serving 6+ months
no-sugar high-iron

Ingredients

  • ½ ripe avocado
  • 1 hard-boiled egg
  • breast milk or formula (optional)

Method

  1. Hard boil egg (10 min), cool and peel
  2. Mash avocado and egg together
  3. Thin with milk if needed
best fresh — avocado browns quickly
Spread on toast strips for finger food version.
