Cheesy Pasta Bake with Chicken & Broccoli
25 min 4 portions 9+ months
freezer-friendly

Ingredients

  • 120g pasta shapes
  • 60g broccoli, broken into florets
  • 80g cooked chicken, cut into strips
  • 200ml crème fraîche
  • 40g cheddar cheese, grated

Method

  1. Preheat oven to 200°C / 180°C fan / Gas 6
  2. Cook pasta according to package, adding broccoli for last 2 min. Drain and set aside
  3. Mix crème fraîche with half the cheddar. Combine with pasta, broccoli and chicken
  4. Transfer to ovenproof dish, top with remaining cheese
  5. Bake 15 min until golden and bubbling
fridge 2 days · freezer 2 months
Tomato, Spinach & Cheese Couscous
25 min 4 portions 6+ months
vegetarian

Ingredients

  • 100g couscous
  • 150ml low-salt vegetable stock
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 medium tomatoes, roughly chopped
  • Handful spinach
  • 30g cheddar cheese, grated

Method

  1. Heat stock and pour over couscous. Cover, leave 5 min, then fluff
  2. Heat oil in pan over medium heat. Add tomatoes, cook 5 min until soft
  3. Stir in spinach until wilted. Add cheese, stir until melted
  4. Mix tomato mixture into couscous. Serve warm or cold
fridge 2 days · freezer 1 month
Mini Courgette & Tomato Pizza Bites
35 min 12 bites 9+ months
finger-food vegetarian freezer-friendly

Ingredients

  • 1 medium courgette, grated
  • 60g self-raising flour
  • 1 egg
  • 30g cheddar cheese, grated
  • 6 cherry tomatoes, sliced
  • 1 tbsp tomato purée

Method

  1. Preheat oven to 200°C / 180°C fan / Gas 6
  2. Squeeze excess water from grated courgette
  3. Mix courgette, flour, egg, and half the cheese
  4. Spoon into greased mini muffin tin. Press down to create bases
  5. Add tomato purée, tomato slices, remaining cheese on top
  6. Bake 15-20 min until golden
fridge 2 days · freezer 2 months
3-Ingredient Pumpkin Orzo
20 min 2 portions 6-12 months
vegetarian freezer-friendly

Ingredients

  • 100g orzo pasta
  • 150g pumpkin or butternut squash purée
  • 30g parmesan, grated

Method

  1. Cook orzo according to package instructions. Drain, reserving some pasta water
  2. Return orzo to pan. Stir in pumpkin purée and parmesan
  3. Add a splash of pasta water to loosen if needed
  4. Serve warm
fridge 2 days · freezer 1 month
Use shop-bought purée for speed, or roast your own squash.
Chicken & Broccoli Rosti
30 min 10 rostis 9+ months
finger-food dairy-free freezer-friendly

Ingredients

  • 1 large potato, peeled and grated
  • 80g cooked chicken, finely chopped
  • 60g broccoli, finely chopped and steamed
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tbsp olive oil

Method

  1. Squeeze excess water from grated potato using a clean tea towel
  2. Mix potato, chicken, broccoli and egg in a bowl
  3. Heat oil in a frying pan over medium heat
  4. Drop spoonfuls of mixture, flatten slightly
  5. Cook 3-4 min each side until golden and cooked through
fridge 2 days · freezer 2 months
Cottage Cheese, Berry & Oat Muffins
35 min 12 muffins 6+ months
vegetarian freezer-friendly

Ingredients

  • 150g rolled oats
  • 200g cottage cheese
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 ripe banana
  • 100g mixed berries (fresh or frozen)
  • 1 tsp baking powder

Method

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C / 160°C fan / Gas 4
  2. Blend oats, cottage cheese, eggs, banana and baking powder until smooth
  3. Fold in berries
  4. Divide mixture into greased muffin tin
  5. Bake 20-25 min until golden and firm to touch
fridge 3 days · freezer 2 months
Great for batch cooking. Defrost overnight or microwave 30 sec.
Rainbow Fritters
20 min 30 fritters 9+ months
finger-food vegetarian freezer-friendly

Ingredients (3 colors)

  • Yellow: 100g sweetcorn, ½ tsp turmeric, 1 egg, 30g flour
  • Pink: 100g cooked beetroot (grated), 1 egg, 30g flour
  • Green: 1 courgette (grated), 50g peas, 1 egg, 30g flour

Method

  1. For each color: mix vegetables, egg and flour
  2. Heat oil in frying pan over medium heat
  3. Drop spoonfuls of mixture, flatten slightly
  4. Cook 2-3 min each side until golden
  5. Drain on kitchen paper
fridge 2 days · freezer 2 months
Make all 3 colors for a rainbow plate! Great for fussy eaters.
